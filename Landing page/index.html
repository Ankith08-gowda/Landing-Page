About Nike

Nike, Inc. is a multinational corporation based in the United States, primarily engaged in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. It is one of the world's leading brands in the sportswear industry.

Our Sport

Nike is a dominant force in global sports, providing footwear, apparel, and equipment across various disciplines, including basketball, football (soccer), running, tennis, golf, and even esports. The brand is the official uniform supplier for major leagues like the NBA, NFL, and MLB, while also sponsoring elite clubs such as Barcelona, PSG, and Liverpool in football. Legendary athletes like Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Cristiano Ronaldo, Serena Williams, and Rafael Nadal have long been associated with Nike, helping to shape its legacy. Nike has also revolutionized sports technology with innovations like Air Zoom, Vaporfly, and Flyknit, giving athletes an edge in performance. It plays a major role in global events like the Olympics and FIFA World Cup, outfitting teams and individuals at the highest level. Beyond traditional sports, Nike has expanded into skateboarding and esports, sponsoring gaming teams and launching streetwear-inspired collections. With a commitment to sustainability and cutting-edge performance gear, Nike continues to lead the sports industry through innovation, branding, and athlete endorsements.
